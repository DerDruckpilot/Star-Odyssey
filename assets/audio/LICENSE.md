# Audio sources and license

The sound effects in `assets/audio/sfx/` are selected, renamed files from the following Kenney packs:

- [Kenney Interface Sounds](https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds)
- [Kenney Sci-fi Sounds](https://kenney.nl/assets/sci-fi-sounds)

Both packs are released under [Creative Commons Zero (CC0 1.0)](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/). Attribution is not required. The original packs identify Kenney as creator and distributor.
